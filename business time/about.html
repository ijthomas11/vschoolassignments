I.T. TACOS

I.T. Tacos was founded in the one and only heart of the "Home of the free and land of the Braves...Atlanta that is. Ish Thomas decided afters years of making homemade tacos and other latin cuisines in the confines of his kitchen to start a local food truck where sold his prized and delicious food to the public. After recieving raving reviews and winning the award for Atlanta's Top Five Food Trucks five years in a row at the local food truck festival, he felt he was only obligated to open an official restaraunt so that he could warm the hearts and bellies of taco consumers nationwide!
